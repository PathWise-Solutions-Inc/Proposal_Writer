REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System Implementation

1. INTRODUCTION
ABC Corporation is seeking proposals from qualified vendors for the implementation of a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. This RFP outlines our requirements, evaluation criteria, and submission guidelines.

2. PROJECT OVERVIEW
We require a modern ERP solution that integrates our financial, human resources, supply chain, and customer relationship management functions. The system must support 500+ users across 5 locations.

3. KEY REQUIREMENTS
3.1 MANDATORY Requirements:
- Cloud-based architecture with 99.9% uptime SLA
- Real-time data synchronization across all modules
- Mobile accessibility for field staff
- Integration with existing Salesforce CRM
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Multi-currency and multi-language support

3.2 OPTIONAL Requirements:
- AI-powered analytics and forecasting
- Advanced workflow automation
- Blockchain integration for supply chain
- IoT device connectivity

4. EVALUATION CRITERIA
Proposals will be evaluated based on the following weighted criteria:
- Technical Solution Fit (30%)
- Implementation Methodology (25%)
- Vendor Experience & References (20%)
- Total Cost of Ownership (15%)
- Innovation & Future Roadmap (10%)

5. BUDGET
The total project budget is between $500,000 - $750,000 USD, including implementation, training, and first-year licensing.

6. TIMELINE
- RFP Response Due: March 15, 2024
- Vendor Presentations: March 25-29, 2024
- Selection Decision: April 5, 2024
- Project Kickoff: May 1, 2024
- Go-Live Target: December 1, 2024
